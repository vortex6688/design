5 Rhythms

5 Rhythms is a dynamic meditation known all over the world, with classes held in NYC down to New Zealand. I wanted to create more dynamic logo that shows of 5 movements and added vibrant colors for a more distinct "punchline" in all the collateral. Working towards, I created a brochure, both print and digital for cross promotion. The apparel shows distinct contrast of black and white for both female /male guests. Check out 5rhythms.com for the best two hours of your life!
